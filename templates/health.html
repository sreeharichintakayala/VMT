HEALTH CAMPS AT RURAL VILLAGES

Vidyaranya Memorial Trust has conducted many Health Medical Camps at various rural villages in collaboration with L.V. Prasad Eye Institute & Kamineni Hospital as part of our "Blindness from the Root Initiative" & General Health Awareness Programmes.

Our Health Camps provide:

  • Screening Tests
  • Distributing Medicines & Spectacles
  • Performing Surgeries free of cost
  • General health check-ups

Vidyaranya Memorial Trust has already touched many lakhs of people in various schools, colleges, government organizations, and different communities within a short span of time.

Cancer from the Root Initiative

The main aim of Vidyaranya Memorial Trust Health Department focuses on the third aspect - Workshops & Seminars to remove "Cancer from the Root Initiative" Awareness Programmes at rural villages & engineering institutions.

  • Educational sessions for poor and needy people
  • Explaining how to guard against these dreadful diseases
  • All services provided completely free of cost
