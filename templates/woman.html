Our Women Welfare Programs

Vidyaranya Memorial Trust is committed to empowering women through comprehensive welfare programs that focus on education, health, skill development, and economic independence.

Our Women Welfare Initiatives

Skill Development

Vocational training programs to enhance employability and self-sufficiency

Health Awareness

Health camps and awareness programs focusing on women's wellness

Education Support

Educational scholarships and support for girls and women of all ages

Our Focus Areas

Health & Wellness

Regular health check-ups, cancer awareness programs, and maternal healthcare services for women in rural areas.

Education

Scholarships, adult education programs, and career counseling for girls and women to pursue their dreams.

Skill Development

Vocational training in tailoring, computer skills, handicrafts, and other income-generating activities.

Legal Awareness

Workshops on women's rights, legal protection, and support systems available for women in need.
