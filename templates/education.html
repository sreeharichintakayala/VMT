VELUGU REKHALU - 2012

Vidyaranya Memorial Trust's second service activity pillar is "EDUCATION" initiated through the program "VELUGU REKHALU-2012", organized annually in January to spread awareness about the importance of education.

The main aim of Velugu Rekalu – 2012 is to provide free education by distributing:

  • Notebooks and Textbooks
  • College Fees and Tuition Fees
  • Bus Passes for transportation
  • Educational materials to poor and needy intelligent students

Achievement Spotlight

One of our brightest achievements is a student who dreamt of higher education and completed a Four-Year B.Tech (IT) program from Koneru Laxmaiah University, Vijayawada.

Vidyaranya Memorial Trust contributed towards his education fund to pursue his academic interests. In his final year of B.Tech, he was selected for Tata Consultancy Services with a package of 4 Lakhs Per Annum and is presently working as a "Software Engineer" at Tata Consultancy Services in Chennai.
